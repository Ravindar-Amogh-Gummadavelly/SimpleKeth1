📄 PRODUCT REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT (PRD)
🌾 Product: SimpleKeth
📌 Version: 1.0 (Final)
1. 🧠 PROJECT OVERVIEW
🔹 Problem Statement

Farmers face price volatility, lack of market intelligence, and dependency on middlemen, leading to:

Distress selling
Lower profits
Uninformed decisions
🔹 Solution Overview

SimpleKeth is an AI-powered platform that:

Predicts crop prices
Recommends when and where to sell
Maximizes net profit (not just price)

It works even in low-connectivity rural environments via:

SMS alerts
Voice support
Offline mode
🎯 Goals
Primary Goals:
Increase farmer income by 15–30%
Enable data-driven selling decisions
Reduce distress selling
Secondary Goals:
Reduce middlemen dependency
Improve accessibility (low literacy users)
Build scalable agri-intelligence platform
